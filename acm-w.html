About Us

The CCET ACM-W was founded in October 2021 with an aim to empower women in the field of computing and increase the global visibility of women in the field of research as well as development. We provide a platform for like-minded people so that they can grow together and contribute to the community in a way that shapes a better world. Our chapter was founded to encourage students, especially women, to work in the field of computing. Inauguration of CCET ACM-W Student Chapter




The CCET ACM-W student chapter was founded in November 2021 with an aim to encourage students, especially women, to work in the field of computing. It was established in accordance with ACM's global initiative to support, celebrate and advocate for engagement of women in computing. The chapter's main goal is provide a global platform for students where they can learn and showcase their skills.
